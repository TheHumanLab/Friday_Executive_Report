WEEKLY OVERVIEW

This week focused on completing the initial operational diagnostics, governance assessment, and structural mapping across MRH Tanzania operations. Findings indicate that the organization remains operationally stable and strategically positioned for scale; however, increasing operational complexity is beginning to exceed the maturity of current workflow visibility, reporting infrastructure, and approval architecture.

Primary implementation efforts are currently centered on governance stabilization, workflow standardization, communication structure refinement, reporting visibility, and foundational operational controls ahead of broader ERP and Odoo integration initiatives.

Current operational constraints appear predominantly systemic and infrastructural rather than personnel-driven, with implementation efforts focused on reducing fragmentation, improving executive visibility, and strengthening scalable governance frameworks capable of supporting long-term regional expansion.
